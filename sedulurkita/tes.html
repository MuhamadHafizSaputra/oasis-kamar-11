Meet The Maker

Ibu Nani Sulistiawati

"It all started with my grandmother's loom. I remember the sound it made, a rhythm that told the story of our family. When I saw the craft fading, I knew I had to do something."

"Rumah Tenun (The Weaving House) was born in 2018 not just to sell products, but to protect a generation's worth of knowledge. I was frustrated seeing cheaper, mass-produced items replace our cultural heritage.[1, 2] My 'why' is simple: to ensure the next generation can feel the pride of this craft."

We started with three weavers in my village. Today, we are a collective of 25 artisans, and every piece we create carries the soul of our community.
